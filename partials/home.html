What is 225 Plus

225 Plus is a site that let's you get all your questions answered directly from the decision makers. 225 plus allows your questions to be directed at the appropriate ministeries. Like minded people can also vote for your question. The officials can then answer the question on this site.

How it works?

Anyone in the general public can ask a question from a ministry, but since they only have limited amount of time, we only direct the questions with over 1000 votes to the appropriate ministeries

How do I create a petition?

You can create a peition and get it answered by other people who needs to get answers to the same problem. But first search to see if a similar question was already asked. Then, you can use the 'create petition' page to start a new petition.
